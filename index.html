Are you experiencing any noticeable hardware issues, like unusual sounds or heating?
Check if other devices can connect to the same network. If they can't, the issue might be with the service provider or the router. If they can, the problem might be with your computer's network settings or adapter.

If your computer isn't displaying any of the mentioned issues, it might be an unidentified software problem or a less common hardware issue. Regular maintenance, updates, and occasional professional check-ups can prevent many problems in the future.
If professional assistance didn't solve the issue, consider whether it's economical to repair or replace the hardware. Sometimes, the cost of repairs can exceed the value or performance gain of new hardware.
